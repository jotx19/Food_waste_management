Join Us in Making a Difference

Be a part of our mission to reduce food waste and promote sustainability. Explore how you can contribute, whether you're a retailer, a consumer, or a charitable organization. Together, we can create a more efficient and equitable food distribution system.

Our vision.

At FWRP, we envision a world where surplus food is transformed into opportunities rather than waste. By fostering collaboration among stakeholders, we aim to create a sustainable ecosystem that supports both people and the planet.

  • Efficient User Registration: Seamlessly create accounts for retailers, consumers, or charitable organizations.
  • Comprehensive Inventory Management: Retailers can easily manage and list surplus food items.
  • Consumer Access to Discounts: Browse and purchase surplus food at reduced prices with notifications on new offers.
  • Charitable Organizations: Claim surplus food from retailers to help those in need.
  • Real-Time Surplus Food Alerts: Receive instant notifications about available surplus food based on preferences and location.
